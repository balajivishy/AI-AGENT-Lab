Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of building machines that can perform tasks requiring human intelligence.

Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of AI that focuses on learning patterns from data.

Deep Learning is a subset of ML that uses neural networks with many layers.

Large Language Models (LLMs) are trained on massive text corpora to predict the next token.

Reinforcement Learning trains agents through rewards and penalties.

AI agents combine LLMs with tools, memory, and planning to perform tasks autonomously.
